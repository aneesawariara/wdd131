Bisanadi National Reserve

A remote and arid wilderness north of Meru National Park, Bisanadi is home to lions, elephants, cheetahs, rhinos, buffalos, and over 400 bird species. Its rivers and bushland offer stunning game viewing, boating, and camping under the stars.

  • Location: Isiolo County, adjacent to Meru National Park
  • Highlights: River Tana & Rojewero, big cats, birdwatching, fishing
